## Copying stacks

When you have a tower of blocks and you want to quickly duplicate the tower to create a wall or other design, you can save time copying the stack.

![single pillar](/assets/stacking1.png)

Using the block placement tool, hightlight the bottom block in the stack.

Shift + Right click (in first person mode) to copy

Hold Z while clicking to paste the whole column
